About this HIT:

  • Please only participate in this HIT if you have normal color vision.

  • It should take about 5 minutes.

  • You will take part in an experiment involving color perception. You'll see a series of pairs of images. In each pair, one image has real colors while the other image has "fake" colors, generated using a computer program. Your task is to determine which image has fake colors. Sometimes the fake colors may look very plausible.

  • You will complete a short practice (less than 1 minute) before starting the main task.
